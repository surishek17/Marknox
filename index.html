Blinkit Clone

Blinkit Clone is an e-commerce platform where users can browse a wide range of products, log in, and log out. Only admins can upload product images, while users can add items to their cart. Key features include user authentication, a searchable product catalog, and an intuitive interface, ensuring a convenient and efficient shopping experience.
